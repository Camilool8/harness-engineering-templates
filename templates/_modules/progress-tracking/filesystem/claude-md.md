## Progress tracking

Work items, plans and status live on disk in `.claude/progress/`:

- **`plan.md`** — the current approach, scope, and decisions made along the way.
- **`tasks.md`** — a markdown checklist of work items.

At the start of any non-trivial task, read both. As you work, keep them current:
tick a task the moment it is genuinely done, add new tasks as they surface, and
append decisions to `plan.md` when you make them. The files should always
reflect reality — a stale checklist is worse than none. See the
`tracking-progress-on-disk` skill.
